Basic to Advanced Level  Hair Course 

Basic Hair Course is a 4-week course. This practical course is created for trainees or hairstylists returning to the profession. This course will refresh their talents and build their confidence with hair cutting. 

Course Details: 

Time: 10am-6pm

 Hairdressers get full knowledge of the basic skills required for cutting. This course gives confidence building as individual students improve at their level. This is an individually evaluated course.

Each week for the first hour, the course begins with a demonstration from the professional tutors (the cut for that week). After the explanation, students will work on their models, and each student is tutored to their ability.

Thermal Hairstyling can be explained as the use of heated instruments on hair. It provides students with advanced training in shampoo, hair and scalp treatments, blow-drying, curling iron, and braiding techniques.
